# DAS-Group-10 
This report looks at how various factors impact the prevelance of obesity in Scotland. The data is taken from the Scottish Health Survey from the years 2008-2012.
We first look at whether the year the study was taken is a good predictor of whether someone is obese or not. 
We then explore the relationship between Obesity and Agegroup, Sex and Education. 
We investigate this using logistic regression.
